About Ytili

Revolutionizing medical donations through AI-powered transparency and blockchain technology

Our Mission

Ytili bridges the gap between medical donors and those in need through a transparent, AI-powered platform that ensures every donation reaches its intended recipient with complete accountability.

Our Vision

To create a world where medical resources are distributed efficiently and transparently, where every donation is tracked from donor to recipient, and where AI helps optimize the allocation of life-saving resources.

Key Features

AI-Powered Matching

Our intelligent system matches donations with recipients based on medical needs, location, and urgency to maximize impact.

Blockchain Transparency

Every transaction is recorded on the blockchain, providing immutable proof of donation flow and ensuring complete transparency.

Mobile-First Design

Optimized for smartphones with real-time notifications and easy-to-use interfaces for donors and recipients alike.
